What do these mean?

We've mentioned all the core motives of Miracle✨. But what do these mean?

  • Personalising Education means that the existing system of generalizing everything needs be put to an end.
  • Students are not products made out of raw materials that coming out of an assembly line🏭 to be labelled QC passed (Like in your mark sheets📄) and to be treated exactly the same way.
  • We're not products📦. We're people. We have different talents🕺🏼, personalities. Maybe this system might suit a 5%, but the world doesn't revolve around, just for those.
  • If we're not supposed to be treated like products, how else should students be treated? A teacher's👨🏼‍🏫 role should not be to state facts that are already in the internet. The Teacher's role should be to find and nourish talents👨🏼‍🎤. Instead of categorizing students in age groups, they should be categorized by their talents.
  • The society doesn't seem to mind us. We're still unknowns. So, we're gonna stage any person who thinks they have something to present or prove to the World.
  • We're gonna promote these people, make them famous. Make them our Identity.The community's Identity.📛
  • We're trying to form more communities around the world that'll center Students👨🏼‍🎓.These communities will work on social issues around the world, whichever seems more pressing at that instant.
  • We're trying to make the whole society Student friendly, instead of being held in the hands of Fat Cats!💰
  • Like we said, we're saving the World😉

    Why is this neccessary?

    Criminal Offence!

    You read it right! You should be aware that killing someone is a criminal offence. The Education System of today clearly kills Students' creativity, talent, confidence, his beliefs. How are the 'F' deciding what the future holds for a Student?! F Grades we mean! A grade is a mere measure of how well can someone cram up in a give period of time. We have seen no practical use in cramming up! Our generation have lost the Joy and Freedom of Childhood, we don't want the next to lose it too.

    If the previous doesn't convince you, think about this. If we have achieved such massive improvement in technology and all other fields just by FORCING kids to stay in some classes that never made sense, what would happen if kids choose to study their own interests?? Students/Kids give back 200% of what's given to them! Be it hate! Be it love!
